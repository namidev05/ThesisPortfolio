Arduino-Based Blind Navigator for Vibes Massage Inc.

aims to help the blind people in navigating while traveling to places

watch live

About

Arduino-Based Blind Navigator is a device that can help the the blind person to travel to their work. It notifies the user where he or she is currently located. The project will also help the blind people to call their relatives or caretakers in case on an emergency.

General Problem

The blind people are having a difficulties going to their work and other places

Vibes Massage are having a difficulties going to their work or ohter places, thus will prefer to travel with family members or caretaker when navigating to unfamiliar places but sometimes without the caretakers they need to communicate alone to reach their destination without knowing the exact location where they are and worrying that might could not be able to arrive at their exact location. Vibes Massage Inc. has many branch, this situation is struggle for them because the blind employees have to travel at desigated branch depend on their given schedule.

Specific Problem

  • The blind people are having difficulties in navigating their location to reach their destination
  • When blind people arrived in unfamiliar places and are in distress
  • caretaker are not readily available all the time to provide assistance

Specific Objectives

  • To create a device that will assist the blind people of Vibes Massage Inc. with directions leading them to other places
  • To create a device that will help the blind people communicate with relatives in case of an emergency
  • To create an application that will aid the blind people to notify and monitor himself by the other where he/she located

General Objectives

To create a device that will guide blind people in traveling

This device will help the blind people in their everyday life especially when they are traveling going to work or other places. it also provides generic direction by converting every word in google map using (text to speech) to the device with an earphone. This device has a GPS installed to the device as a guide for the map location. And the device has also GSM responsible for communication through the blind user and the caretaker, company and family.

Scopes

  • GSM module
  • GPS(to track the blind)
  • Notification
  • Buttons of device(on/off, know location, call for caretaker and company)
  • Maintenance
  • Mobile Application
  • Internet
  • earphone
  • Battery

Limitations

  • Indoor Application
  • limited contact number
  • Obstacles(wall, ramps, stairs and etc.)
  • internet connection is needed
